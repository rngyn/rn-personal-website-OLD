about me

Hey there! I'm Robert, and I'm a developer from Calgary obsessed with creating: Creating new projects, new knowledge, new connections and new life experiences—there's always something new going on with me.

From a young age, I've always had a knack for creativity and problem solving. Development combines these two together so naturally I've grown passionate about it. I truly believe that positive end user experience is a result of thoughtful code. I'm continously learning new stacks and making sure my projects are pixel perfect in function and design.

As a photographer, my camera is always alongside with me capturing life experiences. From seeing the sunset on a rooftop helipad in Bangkok, to eating the greatest hot dog ever in Akureyri. Behind every shot is a story to tell.

I live for constantly shaking things up by pushing my boundaries and escaping my comfort zone. Be spontaneous and try everything—that's my life motto.

Now I turn the conversation to you. I always love meeting new people and hearing their stories, so don't be shy and come say hi!
